Lady Aphrodite

Aphrodite guides love, beauty, and the courage to be vulnerable. She is also a god of war!

Guidance

Try approaching one small social step, compliment someone, join a group, etc.

Execution

This week, attend one event, start one conversation, and follow up with a friend.

Follow-up

Journal how the interactions felt, and note anything that surprised you!

Myth

Born from sea foam, she emerged as the embodiment of love and beauty.

Domains

Love, Beauty, Vulnerability

Symbols

Doves, Roses, Pearls

Offerings

kindness, attention, honesty, and small tokens of affection.
